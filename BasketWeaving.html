    • About Accessible Basket Weaving

    Our basket weaving workshops are accessible to everyone, offering a range of adaptive tools and methods to suit individuals with different physical abilities. The workshops provide a supportive environment where creativity and traditional crafts come together, allowing everyone to participate and enjoy the art of basket weaving.
