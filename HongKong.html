About Hong Kong

Hong Kong, a vibrant, densely populated urban center, is a major port and global financial hub with a skyscraper-studded skyline. It is known for its expansive skyline, deep natural harbor, and extreme population density. The region has a major service economy, characterized by low taxation and free trade. Accessibility in public transport and major tourist attractions is highly emphasized.
