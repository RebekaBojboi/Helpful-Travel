About Istanbul

Istanbul, straddling Europe and Asia across the Bosphorus Strait, is known for its historic sites, including the Hagia Sophia, which has served as a cathedral, mosque, and now a museum. The city offers extensive accessible tourism facilities, including accessible public transportation and guided tours with audio descriptions for the visually impaired.
