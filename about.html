Our Mission

Accessible Travel Guide was created to empower individuals with disabilities to explore the world without limits. We believe in creating an inclusive travel community where everyone can share their experiences, tips, and recommendations for accessible travel destinations.

Why Focus on Accessibility?

Traveling should be a joy accessible to everyone. However, people with disabilities often face barriers that can make traveling challenging. We're here to change that by providing comprehensive information on accessible travel options, accommodations, and attractions around the globe.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of passionate travelers, accessibility advocates, and individuals who believe in the joy of discovery. From professional travel planners to individuals with firsthand experience navigating the challenges of accessible travel, we're united in our goal to make travel more inclusive.
